All-Season Large House

Zarezerwuj online

Home furnishings

Comfortable, large, year-round house, great for family trips and gatherings with friends! It is located on the first shoreline, about 30 meters from one of the cleanest lakes in Poland and about 80 meters from the main beach. Guests have access to the entire house, consisting of 3 two-level apartments (each about 85m2). Each of them has separate, equipped kitchens, bathrooms, and bedrooms. The common area consists of a hall with an additional shared bathroom and a terrace of about 100m2, where guests spend most of their time in the summer. The garden with an area of almost 2000m2 gives children many opportunities to burn their energy. Additionally, in the garden, there is a designated place for a bonfire / fire grill and a small smokehouse. Excellent for family trips with children - throughout the year. Possibility of accommodating more than 12 people - the offer is determined individually.


  • The house is rented exclusively.
  • Feeling of privacy even in a group of 12 people
  • Proximity to a crystal-clear lake
  • Fully equipped kitchen and fire pit/grill
  • Washing machine, dishwasher, WIFI, piano
