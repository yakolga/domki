About us

Learn more about us

We strive to make stays in our homes pleasant. Our houses offer full equipment, except for towels and beach blankets, which you need to bring on your own. We are happy to advise and help in organizing your stay so that each day is different. The Ińsko lake area offers many attractions, so everyone can find something for themselves. Lake Ińsko is distinguished by its large, sandy beach, location in a landscape park, and water transparency of up to 10 meters. The beach has a large pier, outdoor gym, attractions for children (during holidays), rentals of water sports equipment and commercial facilities with ice cream, fries, and other food and beverages. Nearby is a forest where hiking and biking trails take you into a unique nature. The location in the Ińso Landscape Park and Natura 2000 area guarantees a lot of experiences for nature lovers. The surrounding hilly terrain shaped by glaciers, forests, fields, bike paths, many lakes, and water reservoirs ensure satisfaction for active recreation geeks. We encourage fishing, diving, and mushroom picking. Snorkeling is a must in the summer, but for more advanced divers, diving schools operate here all year round. Kayaking trips along the Drawa River are organized not far from Ińsko. They can last for several hours or even several days.

More

Both from the summer house and from the large house, you can walk to Ińsko village along a picturesque path by the lake, passing by the lookout tower built in 2019. There, you will find various shops, restaurants, gastronomic points, and a place where locally caught (but not only) fish is sold - fresh and smoked. The town itself is known for one of the oldest film festivals (since 1973) - Ińskie Lato Filmowe. Despite its evident advantages, Ińsko is not a crowded place, like other holiday resorts. Relax and enjoy the beauty of nature! Among picturesque landscapes, clean water, and fresh air, you can engage in water sports, fish or simply laze on the beach. Experience unforgettable moments and escape from everyday life to the magical place - Lake Ińsko. Book your vacation today!
